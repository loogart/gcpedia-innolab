About

Innovation-driven.

The InnoLab is a proof of concept situated in the Global Affairs Canada’s Information Management & Information Technology Bureau. Supported by a dedicated team of four heros members, our raison D'Être is promoting digital innovation across Global Affairs Canada.

If you see an opportunity to address a departmental challenge through technology, start here! Here’s how it works.

How it works

The diagram below shows the InnoLab process.

Step 1

Start with an idea

Start exploring your idea by accessing our “store-front” of available tools. We have tools for data visualization, crowd-sourcing and prototyping to name a few, with more to be added (or removed) based on your feedback.

Step 2

From Idea to Concept

After exploring your idea, you can contact us! to either begin a prototype, or discuss other digitally innovative initiatives.

Step 3

From Concept to Product

Once your digital innovation becomes a success, we will turn it into a department wide offering. As well, we will work with you to document lessons learned for continuous improvement

Why InnoLab?

Global Affairs Canada employees often have many ideas which, using the right technology, could benefit the department in significant ways. Often times these ideas are never fully explored, primarily because employees do not have a means to expand on their ideas.

For the Information Management/Information Technology Bureau (IM/IT), all projects, big or small, run through the same development process. As a result, smaller projects rarely come to fruition because of the demands of larger, more complex projects being supported. Projects deemed as disruptive, different or innovative rarely succeed because they do not appear to be as important as other, more streamlined proposals.

As a result, the department misses out on a significant amount of potential knowledge employees hold for enhancing core business lines. The IM/IT wants to maximize the knowledge of its departmental staff, no matter their location, by creating an environment for prototyping, incubating and deploying an innovative idea to product.

No matter success or failure, we as a department need to try new things in order to continually improve ourselves!
