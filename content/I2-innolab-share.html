What's Next?

We can publish your digital innovation.

Once your digital innovation becomes as success, we will make it department-wide offering. We will dedicate a maximum 100 days to Build + Launch your concept. We will leverage our expertise in Information Technology to publish your digital innovation.

Visit the About page to learn about how it works!

Launch Criteria

In order to turn your prototype into an initiative for the entire department, please note we will need the following commitment:

  1. You provide a minimum 25% cost to develop the concept for launch.
  2. We consult with the Security and Enterprise Architecture teams.
  3. We ensure the rules related to Privacy and Personal Information are adhered to.
  4. We ensure the Government of Canada procurement guidelines are understood.
  5. We ensure there is an agreed upon approach for addressing the total cost of ownership.

Note: As you see in How it works, your initiative may be developed through the traditional Global Affairs Canada Project Intake Process. This option may be used if your prototype A) Increases in scale/complexity B) Increases in cost and/or C) Increases in Security/Privacy Requirements. Fear not! We will work with you to find the best way forward.
