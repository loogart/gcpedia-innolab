This "User Agreement" is an agreement between you and the Global Affairs Canada and applies to your use of Software as a Service (SaaS) and any related information. It outlines your responsibilities and obligations associated with the use of Software as a Service (i.e. Prezzi, Watson Analytics). Please read this User Agreement and checkmark to acknowledge that you understand and accept the terms and conditions.

By agreeing to the "Terms and Conditions" you are explicitly agreeing that you will not process PROTECTED A, PROTECTED B, or classified information using Innolab procured software.

The InnoLAB retains the right to modify account access to a SaaS at any time. To help mitigate potential compromise, a strong password contains a minimum of 8 characters, including a number, an uppercase letter and a lowercase letter and no more than 3 consecutive identical characters will be provided.
  4. Stolen or Lost Account Access: Users will take all reasonable efforts to protect account access information to the provided SaaS. Lost accounts are to be reported to the InnoLAB Team immediately.
