Need Help?

Let the InnoLab Team kickstart your idea! Together we can build a prototype, crowd-source an idea, or visualize your data. We will dedicate a maximum 100 days to Build + Launch your concept. Visit the About page to learn about how it works!

In order to build your prototype, please note we will need the following commitment:

  1. You’ve thought about your intended goal by completing our Business Case Template.
  2. You have an understanding of the Business & Security Requirements via Initial Documentation.
  3. You have a project champion at Director level and above.
  4. You design an evaluation instrument (i.e. form) to generate feedback with your intended audience.
  5. You provide a failure report if the prototype does not succeed.
